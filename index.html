About us

Joy Bubble® is an artisan bubble tea business with permitted activities to operate as a coffeeshop, cafeteria, food and beverages trading, sweet or snacks preparations duty licensed by the Dubai Economic Department and Dubai Municipality.

Our Mission
& Vision

Mission

To continuously uphold product quality and traceability, trust and sincere co-existence with stakeholders and end-users.

Vision

To be a recognized friendly member of any emerging or stable community by establishing one store at a time.

Business Models

F&B Trading

Joy Bubble® directly imports, exports and is a stockiest of bubble tea products such as raw material ingredients of teas, syrups, flavorings and packaging items imported from China, Taiwan, Vietnam, South Korea and other countries which provides cost reduction for our franchise partners.

Cafeteria

Joy Bubble® serves colorful bubble milk teas loved by any nationalities such as signature series, brulee series, cheesecake or glitter tea series which are uniquely flavored creating sensational market trends, including korean cuisines, pastas, hot foods, pastries, donuts and many more.

Franchise

Joy Bubble® grants and supports franchise opportunities to business-minded stakeholders who wants to replicate its license methods and create a whole new profit stream either as a passive income or a full-time business venture, locally and
internationally.
